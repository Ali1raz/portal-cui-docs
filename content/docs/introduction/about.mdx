## Problem Statement

The existing COMSATS University Islamabad Vehari Campus portal does one thing: let
students register for classes and check results. That is it. Every other administrative
process, leave requests, fee installment arrangements, student complaints, still runs on
paper forms. No tracking, no acknowledgment, no audit trail.

Students submit a form and then wait. Days, sometimes weeks, with no visibility into
where their request is or whether anyone has even seen it. On the staff side, batch
advisors and HODs spend significant time sorting and chasing physical paperwork rather
than making decisions. Documents get misplaced. Requests fall through the cracks and
there is no way to prove what was submitted or when.

This project builds a unified, role-aware portal that replaces those manual workflows.
Leave requests, fee installment splits, and complaints each move through structured
digital approval chains. Every status change is logged. Every actor, student, batch
advisor, HOD, accountant, or admin, sees exactly what they need to act on and nothing
else. The goal is to eliminate the delays, the lost paperwork, and the opacity that the
current system produces as a matter of course.

## Proposed Solution Overview

A contemporary, straightforward, and entirely digital improvement of the current COM-SATS
student portal is the suggested remedy. It presents these new, user-friendly modules. Fee in-
stallments, the leave request system, the announcements module, and student complaints are all
integrated into a single platform. Students can watch announcements online, file complaints,
request fee installments, and apply for leave from any location. They are able to monitor the
progress of requests and receive real-time updates. While accountants can make installments
and produce PDF vouchers, batch advisors and HODs can digitally examine, approve, or reject
requests with remarks. Manual tracking and paper forms are entirely eliminated by this solution.
It increases openness, expedites procedures from weeks to a few hours, lightens staff workloads,
and improves everyone's quality of life at the university.

## Objectives of the Proposed System

Business Objectives (BO):


- BO-1: Create a better student portal that unifies announcements, complaint submission,
fee installment management, and leave requests into a single system.

- BO-2: Reduce manual paperwork and in-person oﬀice visits to increase the effectiveness of
administrative procedures.

- BO-3: Give students a user-friendly online platform so they can make requests and monitor
their progress in real time.

- BO-4: Use a centralized site to guarantee open contact between students and university
management.

- BO-5: Reduce workload and processing time by automating record management and request
handling procedures.
